## 21.0 ETHICAL CONDUCT

## 21.1 Conduct of Supplier

To maintain the integrity of this relationship, Supplier shall not pay or provide either directly or indirectly to any  of  Owner's  employees,  or  nominee  of  such  employees,  any  gifts  or  gratuities  other  than  those  of  a promotional nature with nominal value, including entertainment and free services. Supplier shall promptly disclose in writing to Owner all actual or potential conflicts of interest or financial relationships Supplier may have with respect to, or resulting from, any recommendations or advice given by Supplier to Owner. Supplier shall comply with all ethics policies and procedures established by Owner.
