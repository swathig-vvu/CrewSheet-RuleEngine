## 27.0 SET-OFF AND BACKCHARGES

- (a) Owner shall have the right to apply any monies due to Supplier or any Affiliate of Supplier toward the  payment  of  any  sums  which  Supplier  Group  or  any  Affiliate  of  Supplier  Group  may  now  or hereafter owe to Owner or to any Affiliate of Owner.
- (b) Owner shall have the right to backcharge Losses sustained by Owner caused by Supplier Group's acts  or  omissions  in  failing  to  perform  any  part  of  the  Contract  or  any  Purchase  Order  issued hereunder,  including  but  not  restricted  to,  the  inspection,  transportation,  storage,  erection  or installation of the Work.
