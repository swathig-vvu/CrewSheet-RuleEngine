## Letter of Understanding Re: Training Initiatives

by and between

## Construction Labour Relations - An Alberta Association Mechanical (Provincial) Trade Division

(the 'Association') and

The United Association of Journeymen and Apprentices of The Plumbing &amp; Pipefitting Industry of The United States And Canada, AFL-CIO, CFL, Local Union #488, Edmonton, Alberta,

(the 'Union')

Whereas: The Parties wish to work together to ensure workers provided through the Local Union have all the training and qualifications necessary to be engaged in their trade on worksites throughout the Province of Alberta, and

Whereas: The Parties have established a Joint Educational Trust Fund to provide the necessary training to meet such requirements

## Now Therefore It is agreed as follows .

1. The Educational Trust Fund will provide OSSA Orientation Training, CSTS Training and the Parties will evaluate the need for providing additional safety related training such as OSSA certified Fall Arrest Training, Elevated Work Platform Training, and First Aid Training through the Educational Trust Fund.
2. The Parties agree to make reasonable efforts towards having all required safety related training provided through the training services supplied by the Joint Educational Trust Fund over the course of the current Collective Agreement.  The Parties will meet in April of 2017 or as necessary to discuss the progress of this initiative.
3. The Parties further agree that the Employer shall be responsible to re-certify all expired safety certificates or safety certificates needing renewal due to course content changes, for any employee who has been in their employ for more than sixty (60) calendar days.

## All of which is Agreed to on this\_\_4th\_\_\_day of    MAY , 2025

Construction Labour Relations - An Alberta Association - Mechanical (Provincial) Trade Division

United Assoc. Of Journeymen And Apprentices Of The Plumbing &amp; Pipefitting Industry Local Union #488, Edmonton, Ab.
