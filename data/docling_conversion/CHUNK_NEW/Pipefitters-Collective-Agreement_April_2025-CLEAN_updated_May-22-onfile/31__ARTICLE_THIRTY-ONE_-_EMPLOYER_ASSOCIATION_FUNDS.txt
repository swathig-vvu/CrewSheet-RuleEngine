## ARTICLE THIRTY-ONE - EMPLOYER ASSOCIATION FUNDS

- 31.01 (a) In satisfaction of the Employers' obligations under Section 165 of the Labour Relations Code and in satisfaction of the Employers' obligations under this Collective Agreement, the  Employer  shall  pay  to  the  Association  the  hourly  dues  levied  by  the  Association pursuant to Section 165 of the Code.

The  rate  of  dues  levied  by  the  Association  as  of  the  effective  date  of  this  Collective Agreement shall be seven (7Â¢) per hour for each and every hour worked by Employees of the Employer that are affected by construction registration certificate no. 27 and by this Collective Agreement.  This amount may be amended from time to time, and notice to the Employer of an amendment shall be sufficient to amend this obligation.

In the event of a failure on the part of any Employer to contribute to the Association the dues required to be contributed pursuant to Section 165 of the Labour Relations Code and pursuant  to  this  Article  of  this  Collective  Agreement,  the  Association  may,  at  the  sole
