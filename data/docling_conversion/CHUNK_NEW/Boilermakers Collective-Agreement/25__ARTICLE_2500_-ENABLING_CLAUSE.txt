## ARTICLE 25.00 -ENABLING CLAUSE

## 25.01

Where a particular  Article  or  Articles  of  this  Collective  Agreement  is  or  are  found  to work a hardship for a particular project or specific geographical area, the terms and conditions of this  Agreement for that  project  or  specific  geographical  area,  may  be  modified  by  the  mutual consent  of  the  Local  Lodge  and  the  Boilermaker  Contractors'  Association  when  they  deem  it prudent.  It  is  understood  and  agreed  that  where  mutual  agreement  for  such  change  cannot  be achieved, the request shall not be subject to either grievances or Arbitration.
