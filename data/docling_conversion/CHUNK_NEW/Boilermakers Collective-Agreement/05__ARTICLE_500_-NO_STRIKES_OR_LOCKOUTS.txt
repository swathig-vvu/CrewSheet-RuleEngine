## ARTICLE 5.00 -NO STRIKES OR LOCKOUTS

## 5.01

There shall be no lockout by the Employer and no work stoppages by the Union as required by the Labour Relations Code:

## No Strike unless Permitted

No employees, no bargaining agent and no person acting on their behalf shall strike or cause  a  strike  or  threaten  to  strike  or  to  cause  a  strike  unless  that  strike  is  permitted  by  the Alberta Labour Relations Act.

## No Lockout unless Permitted

No Employer, no Employers' organization and no person acting on their behalf shall lock out  or  cause  a  lockout  or  threaten  to  lock  out  or  to  cause  a  lockout  unless  that  lockout  is permitted by this Act.
