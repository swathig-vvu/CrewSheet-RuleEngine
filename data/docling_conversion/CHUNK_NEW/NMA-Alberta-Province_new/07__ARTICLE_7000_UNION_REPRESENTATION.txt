## ARTICLE 7.000 UNION REPRESENTATION

- 7.100 The designated Union Representative of the Member Union shall be permitted to visit on the job after reporting  to  the  senior  Representative  of  the  Company  on  the  job,  but  will  not  interfere  with  the progress of the work.
- 7.200 Whenever security regulations prevent access to any job or project, the Company or its Representative, will  give  all  possible  assistance  to  the  Union  Representative  in  obtaining  the  necessary  pass  or permission to gain access to such job or project.
